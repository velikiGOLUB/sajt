About Peacocks

Peacocks are large, colorful birds known for their striking plumage and distinctive iridescent tails. They are native to South Asia, but can also be found in other parts of the world.

Peacocks are often associated with beauty, elegance, and grace, and they have long been admired for their stunning appearance.
